Welcome to the CVTC IT Club!

We are a group of students from Chippewa Valley Technical College Information Technology program. This Website is for information about IT Club, upcoming events, and programming resources. Our meetings are every wednesday 4pm - 6pm or 12pm - 1pm at room 152A. Depending on the schedule of the majority, we try to fix the meeting to match everyone's schedule. Make sure you step in one of these days to learn something new, get involved with IT stuff and meet awesome people! hoping to see you there! Happy coding!
