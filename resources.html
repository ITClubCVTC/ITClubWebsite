As developers, we have so many resources available to us that we have no idea existed, below are a few of them used heavily by student, staff at CVTC and also outside programmers included

Brian Hogan's chat room

Brian Hogan is an instructor here at CVTC and tries to help student learn and grow in the field here at CVTC.He has a chat room where any one can pop in and ask questions. Other developerers and students are always present to help also, feel free to log in and ask questions!!

BPHogan.com

Stack Overflow

Great tool for finding out solutions, type in your question and 80% of the time you might find solution to what to want here, We gaurantee!!

StackOverflow.com

Microsoft Developer Network

similiar to Stack Overflow, but ran by Microsoft, Great tool for finding out solutions and new things happening in the microsoft community. If you are a Microsoft lover or you love programming in VB.net, C, C++, ASP.net and the likes of it, this is a sure place for you to visit! Don't miss out on great learning oppurtunites!!
