As developers, we have so many resources available to us that we have no idea existed, below are a few of them used heavily by student / staff at CVTC and also outside programmers included

Brian Hogan's chat room

Brian Hogan is an instructor here at CVTC who tries to help student learn and grow in the field. He has a chat room where any one can pop in and ask questions. Other developers and students are always present to help also if he is unavailable. Great conversations about current trends in the industry occurs here quite often and you don't want to miss out on those! feel free to log in and ask questions!! it is worth it.

BPHogan.com

Stack Overflow

Great tool for finding out solutions, type in your question and 80% of the time you might find solution to what to want here, We gaurantee you!!

StackOverflow.com

Microsoft Developer Network

similiar to Stack Overflow, but ran by Microsoft, Great tool for finding out solutions and new things happening in the microsoft community. If you are a Microsoft lover or you love programming in VB.net, C, C++, ASP.net and the likes of it, this is a sure place for you to visit! Don't miss out on great learning oppurtunites!!

msdn.com

W3C Org

similiar to the above links, The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) is an international community that develops open standards to ensure the long-term growth of the Web. They document standards for HTML and CSS. If you need update regarding latest HTML or CSS change this is a great tool for you.

W3c.org
